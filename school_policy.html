School Policies

Our policies guide discipline, safety, and excellence — creating a supportive learning environment for every child.

Our Commitment

At Shalom International School, our policies are carefully designed to protect students, maintain order, and uphold moral and academic standards. These rules apply to students, parents, staff, and visitors alike.

Attendance & Punctuality

  • Regular attendance is compulsory.
  • Students must arrive on time daily.
  • Parents must notify the school of absences.
  • Medical reports required for extended absences.

School Uniform & Appearance

  • Approved uniform must be worn at all times.
  • Neat grooming and cleanliness are mandatory.
  • Extreme hairstyles and accessories are prohibited.

Classroom Behaviour

  • Respect for teachers and peers is essential.
  • Assignments must be submitted on time.
  • No bullying, fighting, or disruptive conduct.

Code of Conduct

  • Harassment and intimidation are not tolerated.
  • No stealing, vandalism, or dishonesty.
  • Prohibited items are strictly forbidden.

ICT & Device Usage

  • School ICT facilities are for learning only.
  • No cyberbullying or inappropriate content.
  • Personal phones restricted during school hours.

Health & Safety

  • Sick bay available for minor health issues.
  • Parents contacted for serious cases.
  • All medical conditions must be disclosed.

Use of School Facilities

  • Facilities must be handled responsibly.
  • No eating in laboratories or restricted areas.
  • Damage must be reported immediately.

Parent–School Partnership

  • Parents should attend meetings regularly.
  • Open communication is encouraged.
  • Pickup and drop-off times must be respected.

Disciplinary Measures

Violations of school policies may result in:

  • Verbal or written warnings
  • Loss of privileges
  • Suspension or expulsion (for severe cases)
