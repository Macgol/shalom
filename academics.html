Our Academic Programme

A balanced blend of Nigerian and British curriculum designed to nurture confident, disciplined, and globally prepared learners.

Curriculum Overview

At Shalom International School, our academic programme is carefully structured to meet national educational standards while integrating global best practices. We focus on intellectual growth, character development, creativity, and practical skills.

Early Years (Nursery)

Our Early Years programme focuses on play-based learning, creativity, social interaction, and early literacy and numeracy skills in a safe and nurturing environment.

  • Phonics & early reading
  • Numeracy foundations
  • Creative play & music
  • Social and emotional development

Primary School

The Primary School curriculum builds strong academic foundations while encouraging curiosity, confidence, and independent thinking.

  • English Language & Mathematics
  • Basic Science & Technology
  • Social Studies & Civic Education
  • ICT, Creative Arts & Physical Education

Secondary School

Our Secondary School programme prepares students for national and international examinations while developing leadership, discipline, and critical thinking skills.

  • Sciences, Arts & Commercial subjects
  • ICT & digital literacy
  • Career guidance & mentoring
  • WAEC & external exam preparation

Our Teaching Approach

Qualified Educators

Our teachers are trained professionals who are passionate about teaching and committed to continuous development.

Student-Centred Learning

Lessons are designed to encourage participation, collaboration, and critical thinking rather than rote memorization.

Technology Integration

ICT tools are integrated into teaching to enhance learning, research skills, and digital confidence.

Continuous Assessment

We use regular assessments to track progress, identify strengths, and support areas that need improvement.

Beyond the Classroom

We believe education goes beyond textbooks. Our pupils engage in activities that build confidence, leadership, teamwork, and creativity.

Debate & Public Speaking Music & Fine Arts Sports & Physical Education Clubs & Societies Leadership Training Educational Excursions

Preparing Learners for the Future

Our academic programme is designed to equip learners with knowledge, values, and skills needed to excel in secondary school and beyond.
