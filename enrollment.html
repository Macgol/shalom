Welcome to Shalom International School

Choosing the right school is one of the most important decisions a parent can make. At Shalom International School, our admission process is simple, transparent, and supportive.

Why Choose Shalom?

Safe & Caring Environment

We provide a secure and nurturing space where children feel valued and confident.

Strong Curriculum

A blend of Nigerian and British curricula delivered with modern teaching methods.

Qualified Teachers

Passionate educators committed to academic excellence and character development.

Beyond the Classroom

ICT, science labs, music, arts, and extracurricular activities that build confidence.

Who Can Apply?

  • Nursery (Ages 1 – 3)
  • Primary School (Basic 1 – 6)
  • Secondary School (JSS 1 – SS 3)
  • Special Learning Support Classes

Placement is based on age, readiness, and assessment results.

Admission Requirements

Completed admission form
Two passport photographs
Birth certificate or age declaration
Previous school report (if applicable)
Medical fitness report

How to Enroll

1

Pick up or request an admission form from the school office.

2

Submit the completed form with required documents.

3

Your child attends a short assessment or interview.

4

Pay the acceptance and registration fee.

5

Receive uniforms, book list, and resumption details.
