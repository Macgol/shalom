Our Story

A journey of faith, excellence, and commitment to raising confident, disciplined learners since 1997.

Birth of the School

Shalom International School was founded in the year 1997 by Sir (Hon.) Chidi Amadi and Dr. Lady Vidal Amadi, who shared a passion for giving young learners a strong moral and academic foundation.

Starting with just 18 pupils, 4 dedicated teachers, and 4 classrooms, the school was created to provide a nurturing environment where children could grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially.

What began as a simple dream quickly became a mission: to raise a new generation of confident, disciplined, and academically sound children.

The Formative Years

In the early years, Shalom International School focused heavily on strong teaching standards and personalized attention.

  • Academic excellence
  • Character and moral development
  • Clean, safe, and supportive classrooms
  • Strong relationships between teachers, pupils, and parents

These formative years shaped the identity of the school and laid the groundwork for future growth.

Becoming Strong and Stable

As the school's reputation grew, so did its population. The addition of new classrooms, improved learning materials, and more qualified staff helped the school transition into one of the most respected schools in Port Harcourt.

  • Expansion from a handful of learners to hundreds of pupils
  • Recruitment of trained, passionate educators
  • Structured academic programmes aligned with global standards
  • Improved administrative and support systems

This period marked Shalom as a reliable, stable, and well-organized institution.

Growth and Thrive

Today, Shalom International School is a thriving educational institution with modern facilities designed to support holistic learning.

  • Fully equipped ICT centre
  • Modern science laboratory
  • Fine arts and music studios
  • Home economics laboratory
  • Well-stocked library

With over 500 pupils and a strong team of academic and non-academic staff, the school continues to excel academically and socially.

Our Commitment to the Future
