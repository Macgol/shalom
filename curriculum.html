Our Curriculum

A balanced blend of Nigerian and British educational standards, designed to nurture confident, competent, and globally competitive learners.

Academic Excellence with Global Perspective

At Shalom International School, our curriculum is carefully structured to combine the strengths of the Nigerian educational system with international best practices from the British curriculum.

Strong Academic Foundation

We focus on literacy, numeracy, sciences, and critical thinking from the early years.

Global Relevance

Our British curriculum components prepare students for international standards and assessments.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we nurture creativity, leadership, values, and life skills.

Curriculum by School Level

Nursery

Our nursery curriculum focuses on early childhood development through play-based and structured learning.

  • Early Literacy & Numeracy
  • Phonics & Storytelling
  • Creative Arts & Music
  • Social & Motor Skills

Primary

At the primary level, we build strong academic foundations while encouraging curiosity and independence.

  • English, Mathematics & Sciences
  • ICT & Digital Literacy
  • Creative & Performing Arts
  • Moral & Civic Education

Secondary

Our secondary curriculum prepares students for national and international examinations.

  • STEM-focused Subjects
  • British & Nigerian Exam Prep
  • Leadership & Life Skills
  • Career Guidance
